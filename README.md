### **Welcome to the Paked launcher!**



Hello there! I am NovaGames the creator of Paked. Paked is a game bundle full of all of the games I have made, with achievements, rewards and lots of customisation.

I have worked on Paked for a year now, and feel ready to release it. Multiple of my friends and developers have play tested and worked on Paked for ages now and feel like its ready for a proper release.

This is the launcher of Paked, which downloads it and lets you play it. Paked source code will **not** be available here.



#### **Confused why its not working? Here's some ways to solve it.**

Does it show up with an error? Make sure to try



Your device must have 4 or more GB of ram. Lower amounts of ram can cause issues.



Old devices can cause glitches and issues. 



Safari in testing does not work with Paked, try using Google Chrome or firefox.



Phones usually cannot run Paked. Try using a laptop or desktop PC if it does not work.
